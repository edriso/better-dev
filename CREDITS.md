# Credits and sources

The advice in this app is real and attributed. It was gathered from books,
talks, blog posts and widely shared writing by the engineering community. Each
card in the app shows its author and source. This page lists the people and
works the advice draws from.

The wording in the app is kept plain and human. Where a line is a well known
direct quote it stays close to the original. Where it is a paraphrase of a known
idea, it is written in plain language and still credited to the source.

## A note on attribution

A small number of lines describe widely shared engineering practices that do not
trace to a single named author. Those are credited to the community or the
practice itself rather than to a person, so nothing is falsely attributed.

A few sayings that are commonly misattributed online were deliberately left out
or re-labelled to keep the credits honest.
